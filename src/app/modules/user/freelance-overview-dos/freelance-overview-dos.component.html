Do's

  • Attention to Detail: Take your time and effort to create a profile that showcases your skills, experience, and personality in the best light.
  • Credibility and Professionalism: Linking to your relevant professional networks, such as LinkedIn or personal websites/portfolios, adds credibility to your profile.
  • Accurate Skill Description: Be clear and precise when describing your professional skills. Highlight your strengths and expertise.
  • Profile Picture: Adding a professional profile picture that clearly shows your face humanizes your profile.
  • ID Verification: To ensure a safe and secure community for everyone, Skill Mingle may ask you to verify your ID.
